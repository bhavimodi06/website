Chhattisgarh – The Tribal Heart of India

Natural Beauty

Chhattisgarh is home to dense forests, gushing waterfalls like Chitrakote and Tirathgarh, and wildlife sanctuaries. The scenic beauty blends perfectly with tribal simplicity.

Traditions & Culture

The state boasts rich tribal heritage with dances like Panthi, Raut Nacha, and Karma. Bastar Dussehra, a unique and lengthy celebration, reflects their deep-rooted customs and unity.

Cuisine

Chhattisgarhi cuisine includes dishes like Chana Samosa, Faraa, Dubki Kadhi, and Angakar Roti. Rice, pulses, and local herbs define the state's authentic flavors.
