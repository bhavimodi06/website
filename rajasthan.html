Rajasthan - Land of Kings

Cultural Heritage

Rajasthan, known for its royal palaces, deserts, and forts, is a land steeped in history and tradition. Explore the magnificent Amer Fort, the pink city of Jaipur, and traditional music like Ghoomar and Kalbeliya.

Famous Festivals

The Pushkar Camel Fair, Teej, and Desert Festival showcase colorful dances, camel races, and folk performances that mesmerize tourists.

Cuisine

Rajasthani food is rich and spicy. Don’t miss out on Dal Baati Churma, Laal Maas, and Ghevar!
