Madhya Pradesh – The Heart of India

Cultural Heritage

Madhya Pradesh, located in the heart of India, is known for its ancient temples, rich wildlife, and tribal heritage. It’s home to architectural marvels like Khajuraho Temples and UNESCO sites such as Sanchi Stupa.

Famous Festivals

The state celebrates vibrant festivals like Lokrang, Bhagoria, and Tansen Music Festival which showcase classical music, tribal culture, and folk traditions.

Cuisine

Madhya Pradesh cuisine includes dishes like Poha, Bhutte Ka Kees, and Rogan Josh. Local sweets like Jalebi and Mawa Bati are must-tries!
