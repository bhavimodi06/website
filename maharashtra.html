Maharashtra – Spirit of the West Coast

Cultural Heritage

Maharashtra, home to bustling Mumbai and historical Pune, is a land of forts, caves, and classical traditions. Visit the Ajanta and Ellora Caves, explore Maratha forts, and experience the legacy of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.

Famous Festivals

Ganesh Chaturthi is celebrated with unmatched fervor in Maharashtra. Other major festivals include Gudi Padwa, Makar Sankranti, and the Ellora-Aurangabad Festival showcasing classical dance and music.

Cuisine

Maharashtrian cuisine ranges from the spicy Misal Pav and Vada Pav to the sweet Puran Poli. Don't miss local favorites like Pav Bhaji, Modak, and Sabudana Khichdi.
