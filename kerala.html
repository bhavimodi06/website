Kerala – God's Own Country

Natural Beauty

Kerala is known for its serene backwaters, lush greenery, and hill stations. Houseboats and coconut trees define its beautiful landscapes.

Traditions & Culture

Kerala’s traditional arts like Kathakali, Mohiniyattam, and Theyyam are world-renowned. Onam is celebrated with grandeur, featuring Pookalam and Sadya.

Cuisine

Kerala cuisine includes dishes like Appam with stew, Kerala Sadya, Puttu and Kadala curry, and fish-based meals with coconut flavoring.
