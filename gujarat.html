Gujarat – Land of Legends

Cultural Heritage

Gujarat is famous for its temples, crafts, and dance forms like Garba and Dandiya. Sites like Somnath Temple and the Rann of Kutch are key cultural attractions.

Famous Festivals

Navratri is celebrated with vibrant dance and music across the state. Uttarayan, the kite festival, lights up the skies with colorful kites.

Cuisine

Gujarati cuisine is vegetarian and flavorful. Popular dishes include Dhokla, Undhiyu, Khandvi, and Thepla.
