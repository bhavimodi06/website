Bihar – Cradle of Civilization

Cultural Heritage

Bihar, one of the oldest inhabited places in the world, is rich in historical and spiritual significance. It is the land of Gautama Buddha and Lord Mahavira. Major heritage sites include the Mahabodhi Temple, Nalanda University ruins, and Vikramshila.

Famous Festivals

Chhath Puja is the most iconic festival of Bihar, celebrated to thank the Sun God. Other major festivals include Sonepur Mela, Sama-Chakeva, and Pitrapaksha Mela at Gaya.

Cuisine

Bihari cuisine is simple yet flavorful. Litti Chokha is a famous traditional dish, along with Sattu Paratha, Thekua (sweet snack), and Dal Pitha. Must-try delicacies include seasonal sweets like Khaja and Anarsa.
