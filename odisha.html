Odisha – The Soul of India

Cultural Heritage

Odisha is home to one of the most magnificent temple architectures in India. The iconic Konark Sun Temple, Jagannath Temple of Puri, and Lingaraj Temple in Bhubaneswar stand as testaments to the rich cultural heritage of the state. The classical dance form Odissi also originated here.

Famous Festivals

The world-famous Rath Yatra of Puri is a grand celebration that attracts millions. Other important festivals include Durga Puja, Chhau Dance Festival, and Konark Dance Festival.

Cuisine

Odisha's cuisine is simple and flavorful. Try Dalma (lentils with vegetables), Pakhala Bhata (fermented rice), and sweets like Rasagola (claimed to have originated in Odisha) and Chhena Poda.
