Assam – Gateway to the Northeast

Cultural Heritage

Assam, nestled in the heart of Northeast India, is known for its lush tea gardens, wildlife, and spiritual heritage. Explore the famous Kamakhya Temple, Sivasagar’s historical monuments, and the lush banks of the Brahmaputra.

Famous Festivals

The Bihu festival, celebrated thrice a year, marks the Assamese new year and is known for its music, dance, and feasting. Majuli, the world's largest river island, also hosts many vibrant cultural events.

Cuisine

Assamese cuisine is simple yet flavorful. Try traditional dishes like Masor Tenga (tangy fish curry), Khaar, Pitha, and Assam tea – world-renowned for its strong flavor and aroma.
