West Bengal – Culture & Celebration

Cultural Heritage

West Bengal is known for its rich intellectual and cultural heritage. Home to legends like Rabindranath Tagore and Satyajit Ray, the state is a hub of literature, music, dance, and art. Kolkata, the capital, is often called the "Cultural Capital of India".

Famous Festivals

Durga Puja is the most grandly celebrated festival in West Bengal. It reflects devotion, creativity, and community spirit. Other popular festivals include Poila Boishakh (Bengali New Year), Saraswati Puja, and Rath Yatra.

Cuisine

West Bengal is a paradise for food lovers. From the famous Bengali sweets like Rosogolla and Sandesh to spicy fish curries like Shorshe Ilish, the cuisine is delicious and diverse.
