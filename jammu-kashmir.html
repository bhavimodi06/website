Jammu & Kashmir – Paradise on Earth

Cultural Heritage

Jammu & Kashmir is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, vibrant culture, and rich heritage. Explore the famous Shankaracharya Temple, the Mughal Gardens, and the Dal Lake houseboats.

Famous Festivals

Celebrate the traditional festivals of Jammu & Kashmir, including the annual Tulip Festival, Eid celebrations, and Lohri. The region's vibrant folk dances and music like Rouf are also a major highlight.

Cuisine

Experience the delicious flavors of Kashmiri cuisine, including dishes like Rogan Josh, Gustaba, Dum Aloo, and the famous Wazwan feast, served with a traditional Kahwa drink.
