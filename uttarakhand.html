Uttarakhand – Land of the Gods

Cultural Heritage

Uttarakhand is a land of natural beauty and spiritual heritage. The Char Dham Yatra (Kedarnath, Badrinath, Yamunotri, and Gangotri) is a significant pilgrimage. The state is also known for its tranquil hill stations and traditional Kumaoni and Garhwali culture.

Famous Festivals

Festivals like Ghee Sankranti, Harela, and Nanda Devi Raj Jat are celebrated with joy, reflecting the state's deep cultural roots and connection to nature.

Cuisine

Uttarakhand's cuisine is simple yet hearty. Dishes like Aloo Ke Gutke, Bhatt Ki Churkani, and Chainsoo are local favorites. They often use locally grown pulses and grains.
