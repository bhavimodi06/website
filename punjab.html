Punjab – Land of Five Rivers

Cultural Heritage

Punjab is the heart of India’s vibrant culture. Known for its warm hospitality, bhangra beats, and rich heritage, Punjab is home to the iconic Golden Temple in Amritsar and historic sites like Jallianwala Bagh and Wagah Border.

Famous Festivals

Lohri, Baisakhi, and Gurpurab are celebrated with great enthusiasm in Punjab. These festivals are marked by traditional dances, dhol beats, and community feasts.

Cuisine

Punjabi cuisine is rich and flavorful. Savor the taste of butter chicken, sarson da saag with makki di roti, chole bhature, and refreshing lassi. Every bite reflects the love and warmth of Punjab’s people.
